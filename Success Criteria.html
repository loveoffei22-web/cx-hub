Purpose

A single shared tool that lets the Customer Experience team log every customer complaint, route it to the right Responsible Party, and report up to leadership three times a day — replacing scattered WhatsApp messages, Excel sheets and email threads with one cloud-synced source of truth. The app runs entirely on free infrastructure (GitHub Pages + dual Gist sync) at zero hosting cost.

Core Success Criteria

✓
Every complaint is logged in the app within 5 minutes of receipt — no Excel side-files or WhatsApp-only records.
✓
One source of truth — Manager Hub, all 5 Agent Hubs and the Daily Email Report read from the same dual-Gist cloud store.
✓
Every ticket has a Responsible Party assigned before COB — zero unassigned tickets at end of day.
✓
≥ 80 % same-day resolution rate on tickets received in the current business day (rolling 7-day average).
✓
9 AM, 12 PM and 4 PM reports sent to GM on time — generated and pasted into Outlook within 10 minutes of each slot.
✓
Agent slot reports posted to WhatsApp at every Weekday 2 PM / 5 PM and Weekend 1 PM / 4 PM checkpoint.
✓
No data loss — tickets survive browser refresh, device swap and offline gaps (localStorage cache + cloud pull on login).
✓
Daily auto-reset — each agent's ticket store resets at midnight WAT so today's view is always today's work only.
